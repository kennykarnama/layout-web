Our Background

Our company started from a small online store selling security appliances through marketplaces in Indonesia. From this experience, we saw a great demand for affordable yet reliable security devices that provided a sense of security within the household. However, we saw an even greater demand for a more customizable solution as more and more customers came to us not asking about the products that we were selling, but recommendations on what they should buy in order to protect their home from potential security breaches.
It was from these incidental findings that we discovered the average customer to be less knowledgeable on security and smart home appliances. They don’t know what devices they need and even if they do, they would only buy security appliances with conventional technology and difficult installation methods.
This is where we believe Amaan can provide value to our customers. By helping them choose the right smart products for their household, only then will they truly experience living in a smart, secure and comfortable home.
