Mission

Amaan is a smart home company with a mission to improve the safety and living standards of Indonesian homes through integrated smart home solutions. We believe that having complete autonomy over the security and comfort of your home can be done from a single tap of a screen and be available for everyone.

We are able to deliver this promise by providing cutting edge smart home and security appliances that are customized to the needs of our customers' home. In addition, we provide free consultation, fast quotation, in depth risk assessment and options for installation services for a hassle free shopping experience.

Background

Our company started from a small online store selling security appliances through marketplaces in Indonesia. From this experience, we saw a great demand for affordable yet reliable security devices that provided a sense of security within the household. However, we saw an even greater demand for a more customizable solution as more and more customers came to us not asking about the products that we were selling, but recommendations on what they should buy in order to protect their home from potential security breaches.
